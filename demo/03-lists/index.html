How to make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, honey , oat milk and vanilla in a separate bowl
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in two separate batches, mixing throughly
  4. Distribute mixture into greased muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 minutes , or until browned
  6. Enjoy!
